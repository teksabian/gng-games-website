For Your Guests

How Players Join

Your guests don't need to download anything, create an account, or ask anyone for help. Here's their entire experience:

1 📷

Scan the QR Code

Every table gets a card with a unique QR code. Scan it with any phone camera — it opens right in the browser.

2 ✏️

Name Your Team

Pick a team name and you're instantly connected to the game. That's it — you're playing.

3 🎮

Answer on Your Phone

Questions appear each round. Type your answers, hit submit, and wait for the results. Answers auto-save if you switch apps.

4 🏆

Watch the Scoreboard

Scores update live after every round. Cheer, groan, trash-talk the table next to you. That's the whole point.
